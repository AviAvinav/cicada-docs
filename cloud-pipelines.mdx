<Warning>Cloud deployments are coming soon </Warning>


### 1. Sign up to Cicada

Sign up at [cicada.build](https://cicada.build/signup)

### 2. Link your GitHub repository

Connect your org's GitHub account and choose the repository you want to connect with.

### That's it!
Now whenever you / your teammates create a PR, Cicada will comment on it with the status of your pipeline.
